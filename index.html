Cheeseburgers!

Best Topping Options (This list is just what goes good on burgers independently from other ingredients.)

  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayo
  • Pickles
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Bacon (Don't overuse it)

What to do

  • Season the meat with something so its not bland.
  • Make sure that the meat isn't dry after cooking,
    you want it moist.
  • Warm up or toast the bun. Noboby likes cold buns.

What not to do

  • Don't just put raw ground beef in a pan and
    call it a burger patty.
  • Don't overload the burger with a lot of ingredients.
    It gets rid of the burger taste.
  • Be careful when using bacon or cheese because
    too much can make it overly greasy.
